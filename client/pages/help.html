Frequently Asked Questions

A great profile includes clear, recent photos that show your personality, an engaging bio that highlights your interests and what you're looking for, and honest information about yourself. Make sure to complete all profile sections and keep your information up to date.

When you and another user both like each other's profiles, it creates a match! You can then start messaging each other. Our algorithm shows you profiles based on your preferences, location, and compatibility factors.

Always meet in public places for first dates, tell friends about your plans, trust your instincts, never share personal information too quickly, and use our in-app messaging before sharing your phone number. Report any suspicious behavior immediately.

You can block and report users who make you uncomfortable. Go to their profile, tap the menu button, and select "Block" or "Report." We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

You can delete your account by going to Settings > Account Management > Danger Zone > Delete Account. Please note that this action cannot be undone and all your data will be permanently removed.

Consider updating your photos with recent, clear images, expanding your discovery radius, adjusting your age preferences, completing your profile fully, and being more active on the app. Quality profiles with genuine content tend to get more matches.
